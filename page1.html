Finite Element Analysis on One Dimensional Steel Bar

Course- Finite Element Analysis (Spring 2018)

This study considers 1-D bar which is fixed at one end and the stress is provided at the other hand, using Finite Element Method and MATLAB one can find displacement and stress at each node of the 1-D bar. Finite Element Method divides any part into a finite number of nodes depends on geometry the shape of the element can decide. In Finite Element Method governing equation need to deiced based on the problem and then convert strong form of the equation to the weak form by using weighting function and boundary condition. Using Galerkin approximation Method stiffness, force and mass matrix can form and Gauss quadrature integration method is used for the integration. Using this matrix we can do static, modal and dynamic analysis on the bar.

 Heat Transfer & Thermal Flux Analysis of Composite Cylindrical Pressure Vessel 

Course- Computational Methods in Engineering (Fall 2017)

This study mainly consists of the thermal flux analysis of the composite cylindrical pressure vessel made of two materials. The study uses a computational method for the analysis of the heat flux in the cylinder. One of the main advantages of computational methods is its use in analyzing the real-world physical structures and changes in its behavior depending upon the change in itâ€™s surrounding and/or change in the parameters affecting its properties. Implementation of Automated Manual Transmission [AMT] in Two-Wheelers 

Senior Design Project -Undergrad ( Spring 2016)

In a world shifting to automatic and gearless transmission technologies from manual systems, we intend to change the current scenario of two-wheelers dominated by Continuously Variable Transmissions (CVTs) & Manual Transmissions (MTs) by implementing the concept of Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) in Two Wheelers. An AMT is an automobile transmission that changes gears automatically, facilitating manual gear changes by removing the need to press a clutch pedal at the same time as changing gears. A combination of Electronic and Mechanical Systems make quick, smooth gear shifts. Closed-loop systems & fuzzy logic are used in this project, to calculate how and when to automatically change gears in the vehicle for optimum performance, fuel economy and shift quality. The biggest advantage of an AMT gearbox is the price advantage it offers over AT since the base transmission is the same as a manual transmission..

Portable Conveyor Base Load Lifting Mechanism

Junior Design Project -Undergrad (Fall 2015)

 In today's developing world, material handling is heavily dependent on manual labor, which plays a vital role in small-scale manufacturing industries. In our project we aim to design a conveyor based load lifting system, reducing the manual labor required for material handling. The system can handle material from points at different elevations, like storage shelves, trucks, trailers, etc. The mechanism is portable and flexible, with an adjustable height of the platforms to cater to the needs of a variety of users and locations. Material handling is done by a cleated rubber conveyor belt powered by variable speed reversible electric motor. The system folds into a single platform hence reducing the space required for storage. A wheel and brake arrangement allows for easy movement during transporting the system, as well as serves as rigid support during operating conditions of the system. The system is fabricated with a small & compact frame and is capable of lifting a load of up to 100 kg.
